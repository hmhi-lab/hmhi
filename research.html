Our Research

Human-Centered AI

Understanding human cognition, trust, and decision-making to design AI systems that align with human needs in high-stakes environments.

Resilient Infrastructure Systems

Developing adaptive, data-driven tools and decision support systems for infrastructure maintenance, safety, and coordination.

Human-AI Collaboration

Exploring collaborative reasoning, feedback loops, and team mental models between humans and AI in complex environments.

Immersive Training & Simulation

Building VR-based training platforms and AI avatars to enhance skill transfer, scenario-based learning, and cognitive preparedness.

Our projects span a wide range of domains, including nuclear energy, transportation, construction, and remote infrastructure. Our work combines human factors, cognitive modeling, AI reasoning, and systems engineering to improve safety and collaboration at every level.
