About Us

HMHI (Human-Machine Harmony for Infrastructure) Lab is based at Carnegie Mellon University. Our research focuses on improving the harmony between human decision-makers and intelligent systems, especially in the context of civil and nuclear infrastructure.

Research Areas

  • Human-in-the-loop AI for infrastructure operations
  • Explainable AI and trust in automation
  • Fault detection, diagnosis, and recovery (FDIR)
  • Knowledge graph and RAG systems for safety-critical tasks
  • Tacit knowledge transfer through VR and multimodal learning
