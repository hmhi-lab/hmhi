Pingbo Tang

Associate Professor, Civil and Environmental Engineering

Dr. Pingbo Tang is a Professor in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. His research focuses on the sensing, modeling, analysis, and visualization of civil infrastructure systems to support decision-making for construction, operation, and maintenance. He specializes in reality modeling, information modeling, and uncertainty modeling of complex systems, and develops methods that integrate human-in-the-loop sensing and automation.

Dr. Tang has received multiple best paper and service awards from ASCE and TRB. He serves on editorial boards of key journals in computing and infrastructure engineering, and actively contributes to advancing smart infrastructure systems through academia-industry collaboration.
