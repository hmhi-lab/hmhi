What We Work On

Human-Centered AI

We study how AI systems can be designed to align with human decision-making, trust, and cognitive processes in high-stakes environments.

Resilient Infrastructure

We develop data-driven and human-in-the-loop approaches to support safe, adaptive operation of critical infrastructure systems.

Human-AI Collaboration

We explore ways to enhance collaboration between people and machines through explainability, feedback loops, and task-sharing frameworks.

Training and Simulation

We design immersive learning environments using virtual reality and AI avatars to train and support frontline infrastructure operators.
