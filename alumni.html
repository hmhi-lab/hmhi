Alumni

Meet the former members of HMHI who have contributed to our mission and gone on to pursue exciting new endeavors in research, industry, and beyond.

Professor Robert "Bob" Thunderpants

robert.bob@alumni.edu

Bob received his Ph.D. in 1985 and now works as a senior researcher in cloud-powered sandwich making technology. At HMHI, he was instrumental in developing the "AI-Powered Coffee Maker 3000" prototype.

Maria "The Multitasker" Sanchez

maria.themultitasker@alumni.org

Maria completed her MS in 2021 and currently works as a data scientist in the "Hologram Pizza Delivery" industry. At HMHI, she contributed to simulation-based pizza delivery modeling.

Dr. Jamal "The Algorithm Whisperer" Lee

jamal.thewhisperer@alumni.net

Jamal worked on the world's first decision support system for choosing which pizza to order. He now serves as a professor in the Department of AI and Pizza Studies at the University of Holograms.
