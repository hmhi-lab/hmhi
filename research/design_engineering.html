HMHI Human-Machine Harmony for Infrastructure

Enhancing Communication Between Designer and Engineer in Building Design Process

Building or infrastructure design process requires participation and collaboration of multiple stakeholders, particularly those of designers and engineers.

However, each stakeholder’s different language and different priorities make it challenging to communicate with each other and reach common objectives.

To address this, we aim to identify the key bottlenecks where miscommunication occurs most frequently, understand the root causes, and develop an assistant system to support effective communication among stakeholders.
