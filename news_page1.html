Cheng Zhang’s ENG-DRB Dataset

ENG-DRB is a discourse relation dataset built specifically for engineering content, introduced in a recent paper at IJCNLP-AACL 2025. The dataset analyzes over 1,200 relations extracted from real tutorial videos across civil, mechanical, and electrical engineering and shows that even advanced AI models struggle to accurately understand engineering discourse, particularly implicit causal and temporal relations. This work highlights a gap between general-purpose NLP and the specialized language used by engineers. The dataset and code are open-source and publicly available.
